About Tim

Through working in the fitness industry for the past 8 years, I found a passion for helping people change their lives through health & wellness. As the Club Manager, I was responsible for overseeing 25+ employees. As the Regional Training Manager, I was also responsible for training newly hired & promoted Managers, as well as the career development of Assistant Managers. As a leader, I strived to create an environment where people could be themselves & grow, while enjoying their time at work.

Despite also being a sports fan, I've always been a nerd at heart. My love of video games & technology motivated me to take the leap to change fields. Learning how to code has already been a rewarding journey that's challenged me to think more strategically, analytically & creatively. Becoming a Full Stack Developer is my dream that I work hard everyday to make reality.
